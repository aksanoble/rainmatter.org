Who are we?

Rainmatter Foundation is an initiative by the team behind Zerodha, India's largest stock broking firm. With the understanding that climate change is not a technological problem to be solved like in our line of work, we have committed to using the resources available to us to support efforts in the areas of climate change, environmental conservation and rejuvenation, and sustainable livelihoods. Funding

We provide funding through investments, grants and fellowships to organisations and individuals working on environment, climate advocacy, policy and research.
